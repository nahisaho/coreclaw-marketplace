## Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB)

### Cost Classification
| Category | Definition | Action |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Must Have | Legally or contractually required | Maintain at minimum |
| Should Have | Needed for competitive parity | Optimize efficiency |
| Nice to Have | Beneficial but not essential | Justify or eliminate |
| Eliminate | No strategic value | Remove immediately |

### Application Rules
1. Every cost item starts at zero — must be justified to be included.
2. "Must Have" does not mean "at current level" — minimize even required costs.
3. Savings from elimination should be reallocated to growth investments, not just cut.
